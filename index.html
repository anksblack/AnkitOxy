About

How did we started?

  • Year 2020

    Covid-19 first wave

    Although the strain of COVID in our country started back in the month of March 2020, and we were able to provide necessary food supplies to the needy all thanks to the efforts of people like you.

  • March 2021

    Second wave of pandemic

    It wasn't like we should have let go our masks this soon. So why? A complete breakdown of our health structure, no oxygen, no hospital beds !!

  • May 2021

    Lets Beat Covid!

    Well we know it's a tough time and we are again stuck in homes but instead to sharing posts can we help spread a word that can save lives? That's why lets gear up and help all the needy again. WE CAN DO IT!
